What makes NIMT standout?

NIMT was established in 1987. With a legacy of over 33 years, NIMT offers 21 Higher Education Programs at 11 Colleges across 5 Campuses in Ghaziabad UP, Greater Noida UP & Kotputli Jaipur. Disciplines include Law, Management, Teacher Training, Medicine, Biotechnology, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Arts, Science & Commerce. NIMT also offers schooling through its two schools in Ghaziabad UP. NIMT also has new-age online education programs for working professionals. NIMT is also working in the skill development sector & placement of rural unemployed youth in the country. The Institutions are top-ranked in their categories and strive to deliver excellence with the motto “Move Forward”.
